#  Financial Survival Toolkit for Women-Owned Micro Businesses (South Africa)
A practical toolkit to help women-owned micro businesses in South Africa manage finances, track expenses, forecast revenue, plan growth, and access resources to build sustainable, thriving businesses.

## Overview
The financial survival toolkit is designed to empower women entrepreneurs in South Africa by providing practical financial tools, templates, and guides. It helps micro business owners manage cash flow, plan budgets, forecast revenue, and make informed decisions to grow sustainably.

## Features
- Budgeting and cash flow management templates  
- Revenue forecasting and expense tracking tools  
- Guides for funding, investment, and financial planning  
- Educational resources to improve financial literacy  

## Who is this for?
- Women-owned micro businesses in South Africa  
- Startups and small-scale business owners seeking financial guidance  
- NGOs and support organizations assisting women entrepreneurs
